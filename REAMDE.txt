You are currently viewing the official audio project files for the HTL Arcade.
If you're wondering what .cwp(cake-walk-project) files are, every piece was made using CakeWalk Sonar as DAW.
You will find finished and exported projects in the corresponsive "Audio Export" folder of every project.

You can get MIDI files through opening the desired project in CakeWalk and exporting as MIDI file.
